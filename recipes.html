Grilled Cheese

Origin: American Source: Own Recipe Category: Lunch

My mom made this many times as a child and it always reminds me of home when I make it now.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter

Recipe Steps

  1. 1. Heat a griddle or any pan large enough to comforatbly fit two pieces of bread
  2. 2. While it's heating, spread a thin layer of butter on one side of both pieces of bread
  3. 3. Place the buttered side down in the hot pan and your slices of your cheese on the bread
  4. 4. Once the cheese starts to bubble, flip the pieces of bread together to create your sandwich
  5. 5. Toast to taste

Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Origin: American Source: Delish.com Category: Dessert

A classic dessert fairly healthy dessert everyone will love. A great recipe to use for those overripe bananas.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 cups (240 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 large egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup chopped toasted walnuts
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Recipe Steps

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 350ยบ. Line a 9"-by-5" loaf pan with parchment and grease with cooking spray.
  2. 2. In a medium bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. 3. In a large bowl, mix egg, egg yolk, granulated sugar, butter, sour cream, and vanilla. Add bananas and stir until combined. Gradually add dry ingredients to banana mixture until just combined.
  4. 4.Fold in walnuts and chocolate chips and transfer to prepared pan.
  5. 5. Bake bread until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cool 10 minutes in pan, then invert onto a wire rack and let cool completely.

Spaghetti

Origin: Italian Source: Own recipe Category: Dinner

A cheap, easy, and quick meal that's perfect for after a long day at work.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Dry spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese

Recipe Steps

  1. 1. In a pan, add the pasta sauce and just enough water to cover the bottom of the pan. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  2. 2. Add the spaghetti noodles to the pan and cook according to the packaging instructions.
  3. 3. In a large bowl, mix egg, egg yolk, granulated sugar, butter, sour cream, and vanilla. Add bananas and stir until combined. Gradually add dry ingredients to banana mixture until just combined.
  4. 4. Once the noodles are cooked, plate on a pan and add cheese on top.
